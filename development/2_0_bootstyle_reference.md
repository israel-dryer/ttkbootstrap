## Grammar

A bootstyle is `[color-][modifier-]<base-type>[-orient]`, with exactly
one token per slot in that order. The **base-type** is normally
inferred from the widget and only spelled for the chameleon families
`toggle`/`toolbutton`. Unknown tokens fail loudly (a warning by
default; `set_bootstyle_strict(True)` or `TTKBOOTSTRAP_STRICT=1` raises).

## Colors

`primary`, `secondary`, `success`, `info`, `warning`, `danger`, `light`, `dark`, `neutral`
